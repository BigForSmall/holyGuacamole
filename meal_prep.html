Holy Guacamole

Save lots of time dedicating just an hour a week for meal preparation!

Trick #1

Make sure to wash and slice cabbage, carrots, cucumbers, celery and anything you can in advance.

Tip: Store in a container in the fridge.

Trick #2

Keep your cheese grated. This way you will have a perfectly balanced taste of pasta or salad in no time.

Tip: Store in a plastic zip-bag in the fridge.

Trick #3

Marinade your meat not only with salt and pepper. But also with garlic, onion, tomato and other spices.

Tip: Store in a plastic zip-bag in the fridge.

Trick #4

Do not forget to add some magic to your fish. Salted fresh or baked in the oven fish in a blink of an eye.

Tip: Store only for two days in container in the fridge.
